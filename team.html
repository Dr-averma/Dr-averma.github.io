Principal Investigator

Dr. Shuvajit Mukherjee

Assistant Professor & Principal Investigator

Dr. Shuvajit Mukherjee commenced his tenure as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil and Infrastructure Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur in February 2024. His research group is dedicated to exploring the fields of metamaterials, wave propagation, computational mechanics, and sandwich composites. His findings demonstrate that geometric curvature can be precisely tuned to manipulate frequency bandgaps, thereby enhancing vibration isolation capabilities.
