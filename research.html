Our Focus Areas

Frontiers of Mechanics

Investigating the intersection of structural geometry and physics to engineer the next generation of intelligent materials.

Metamaterials

Designing architected lattices with negative Poisson's ratios and vanishing shear modulus. We focus on how sub-wavelength geometry dictates macroscopic behavior.

Wave Propagation

Harnessing frequency bandgaps and topological protection to control elastic waves. Our work enables vibration isolation and acoustic energy harvesting.

Sandwich Composites

Engineering ultra-lightweight structural panels with high stiffness-to-weight ratios. We explore curved cores and multi-functional core geometries for aerospace applications.

Computational Mechanics

Developing high-fidelity numerical solvers using Julia and FEniCS. Our simulations solve complex nonlinear elasticity and dynamic instability problems.

Our lab is always looking for motivated students and industrial partners to explore new horizons in wave mechanics.
