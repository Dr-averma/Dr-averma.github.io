Research Frontiers.

We operate at the intersection of geometry and physics, engineering the next generation of architected solids and wave-controlling structures.

01

Metamaterials

We design architected materials with properties not found in nature. By manipulating the micro-scale topology, we create solids with negative Poisson's ratios, programmable stiffness, and extreme energy absorption capabilities.

  • Auxetic Structures
  • Topological Optimization
[Structural Lattice Visualization]
[Bandgap Frequency Analysis]
02

Wave Propagation

Investigating how elastic waves travel through periodic media. Our focus lies in Bandgap Engineering—creating frequency filters that can effectively block or redirect vibrations and acoustic energy for seismic protection and stealth.

  • Phononic Crystals
  • Vibration Isolation
03

Sandwich Composites

Developing high-strength, low-weight hybrid structures. We explore core architectures that maximize structural integrity while providing multifunctional benefits like thermal insulation and impact resistance.

  • Honeycomb Cores
  • Debonding Analysis
[Composite Layering Model]
[FEA Mesh Simulation]
04

Computational Mechanics

Utilizing advanced Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and custom Julia-based solvers (Gridap.jl) to simulate complex non-linear behaviors. We bridge the gap between theoretical mathematics and physical reality through high-fidelity modeling.

  • Julia Computing
  • Non-linear FEM
