About the Laboratory

The Laboratory conducts cutting-edge research in metamaterials, wave propagation, and computational mechanics. We focus on the design of lightweight, tunable, and intelligent materials with enhanced performance.

Our major research thrust lies in developing novel lattice-based metamaterials, including hexagonal and architected cellular structures.
