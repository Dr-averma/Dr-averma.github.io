About the Laboratory

The Advanced Materials & Wave Mechanics Laboratory (AMWM Lab) conducts cutting-edge research in metamaterials, wave propagation, and computational mechanics.

Our work focuses on the design of lightweight, tunable, and intelligent materials with tailored mechanical and dynamic properties.

Major research thrusts include architected lattices, phononic crystals, bandgap engineering, nonlinear dynamics, and multiscale computational modeling.

Research Highlights

Metamaterials

Design of re-entrant, anti-chiral, and programmable lattices for wave manipulation and vibration isolation.

Wave Mechanics

Bloch analysis, bandgap engineering, dispersion tailoring, and nonlinear wave propagation.

Computational Modeling

Finite element methods, phase-field fracture, multiscale homogenization, and topology-driven design.
